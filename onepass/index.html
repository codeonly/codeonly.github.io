QUALITY SECURITY

Secure with SHA 256 bits hash and AES algorithm. Never store your password or secure data in plain text. Automatic lock if you leave the App or after a set time from your last activity.

User Guide

First time you need to create master password, make sure your remember this, otherwise can't access your saving.



In category or item list, you can swipe left to update or delete.



In item list, you can search by keyword to quickly find your saving.



Say goodbye to think a strong password, just click generate button, then it's done.
